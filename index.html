Our Vision & Mission

Bachhpan is a student-run organization who provides basic education for free to underpriviledged kids who can't even get basic education. We at Bachhpan help them achieve their dreams and shapes them as they progress in their lives. Currently, more than 350+ students gets their basic education at our pathshaala on daily basis. There are in total 5 pathsaala running as of in year 2023. 3 in anand and 2 in Ahemdabad.

    • Our Projects

    Under Bachhpan there are total 4 projects running:

    1) Pathshaala: In Pathshaala project we teach kids daily 1 hour just like our ancient times where a guru sits under the Banyan tree and students sits in front of him to get education. Just like that we teach students in our Pathshaala project

    2) Aadhar: In this project, we select some of the greatly talented students but with very poor financial condition or if they are working as child labour. In Aadhar we literary become their second parents and help them with their every need.

    3) JNV:JNV (Jawahar Navoday Vidhyalay) is a scholarship exam from CBSE board. If cleared by students they get free education from standard 5th to 12th from JNV school. In this project we prepare 4th standard students for this exam.

    4) ABCD: We believe kids should also get a chance to read different types of books such as biographies and autobiographies of great leaders, etc. In ABCD project, we provide them various types of interesting books for them to read.
